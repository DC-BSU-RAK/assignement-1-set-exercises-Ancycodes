Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a science fiction horror series set in the 1980s in the town of Hawkins, Indiana. The story begins with the mysterious disappearance of a young boy, Will Byers, and the appearance of a girl with supernatural powers known only as Eleven. As friends and family search for Will, they uncover government experiments, alternate dimensions, and terrifying creatures.

Key Information

  • Genre: Sci-Fi, Horror, Thriller
  • First Released: July 15, 2016
  • Seasons: 4 (as of 2024)
  • Created by: The Duffer Brothers
  • Streaming on: Netflix

Cast & Creative Team

Main Cast:

  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Finn Wolfhard as Mike Wheeler
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • Gaten Matarazzo as Dustin Henderson

Creators: Matt and Ross Duffer

Main Characters

  • Eleven: A girl with psychokinetic abilities.
  • Mike Wheeler: One of Will's friends and Eleven’s close companion.
  • Jim Hopper: The town’s chief of police who becomes Eleven’s guardian.
  • Joyce Byers: Will’s determined and caring mother.
